## LIKELY-FABRICATED

- `kraft2021democratizing` — DOI `10.1016/j.jfineco.2021.02.006` actually resolves to "Are disagreements agreeable? Evidence from information aggregation" (JFE 2021), NOT "Democratizing Credit: Alternative Data and Consumer Lending" by Kraft, Chen, Dong, Sauermann. No paper with this title and author set exists in JFE or in Google/Scholar searches. (searched: Google for title + JFE; DOI resolution; author combinations)
- `ooms2013lookahead` — "Lookahead Bias in Empirical Finance" by Ooms & Jansson, J. Empirical Finance 42, 99-112, 2017. DOI `10.1016/j.jempfin.2017.02.004` returns HTTP 404. Journal of Empirical Finance vol 42 never existed with these page numbers (the journal was at vol 40-44 in 2017; vol 44 Dec 2017 TOC does not contain it). No Google Scholar hits for the exact title. (searched: DOI, exact title in JEF TOC, Google Scholar)
- `navas2022optbinning` — DOI `10.1287/ijoc.2021.1151` resolves to "Branch-and-Price Approaches for Real-Time Vehicle Routing with Picking, Loading, and Soft Time Windows" (IJoC 2022), NOT the Navas-Palencia optimal-binning paper. An underlying Navas-Palencia arXiv preprint exists (`2001.08025`), but no INFORMS J. on Computing publication with title "Optimal Binning: Mathematical Programming Formulations for Binary, Continuous and Multiclass Targets" vol 34(3) 1419-1442 appears to exist. Volume/pages/DOI are fabricated attribution. (searched: DOI resolution, INFORMS site, arXiv, Google Scholar)

## UNCERTAIN

- `agarwal2020fintech` — Paper ("Financial Inclusion and Alternate Credit Scoring for the Millennials") is real as SSRN/ISB working paper, but DOI `10.1287/mnsc.2023.4878` actually maps to "A Two-Step Synthetic Control Approach" (Li & Shankar). Cannot confirm published Management Science placement at vol 70(8), 5237-5259. Wrong DOI (or paper not yet published there).
- `hurlin2024fairness` — Paper "The Fairness of Credit Scoring Models" is real (Hurlin/Pérignon/Saurin), but the cited DOI `10.1287/mnsc.2023.4812` is wrong. Actual published DOI is `10.1287/mnsc.2022.03888`, and published in Management Science vol 72 (not 70).

Possible DOI-field error (or alternate reprint), not necessarily fabrication.
